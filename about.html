Est. 2010

A Decade of
Fire & Flavour

Spice & Ember began with a single wood-fired grill and a simple belief: that fire transforms food into something primal and unforgettable. What started as a small neighbourhood kitchen has grown into one of the city’s most celebrated dining destinations.

Today, our chefs continue to honour those roots — sourcing the finest seasonal ingredients and cooking them the way nature intended: over open flame.
